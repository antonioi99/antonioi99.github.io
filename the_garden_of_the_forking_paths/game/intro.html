Welcome to The Garden of Forking Paths

An Interactive Narrative Experience

About the Game

This serious game is inspired by Jorge Luis Borges' short story The Garden of Forking Paths.

You will take on the role of Yu Tsun, a spy navigating a philosophical labyrinth where choices define your path.

You will start yout journey like in the original short story

However, you will have the possibility to choose everytime between different options that will define your own story, leading to a different end of the story.

The game style is rather simple:

  • every step you take in your journey is associated with a different part of the story;
  • you have the possibility to move in four directions by clicking on the bottoms on the left side;
  • after you have choosen where to go the dot in the middle will move consequently and another part of the story will revealed to you;
  • from time to time instead of moving in some direction you will be asked to choose between a couple of alternatives;
  • the progress bar on the right show how far you are from a node called 'The point' from which different endings of the story are possible.

If you want to have a look at the structure of the story, you can click on "View the labyrinth map".
